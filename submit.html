Instructions

We welcome submissions for Literature (Fiction, Nonfiction, and Poetry) and Art (Cover Art with runner-up submissions as Featured Art).

Literature Submissions

Literature submission cycles are six weeks long (currently 7/15–10/26). We select 10–15 pieces of Fiction, Nonfiction, and Poetry based on insight, commentary, and quality. We encourage artistic liberty and authentic thinking!

Art Submissions

Art submission cycles last two weeks (next open for the Issue 2 Cover from 7/15–10/26). We select an art piece to be featured on the cover of the issue and several other to be featured throughout the work. This will appear permanently on the website.
