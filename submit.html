SUBMIT

INSTRUCTIONS:

We welcome submissions for Literature (Fiction, Nonfiction, and Poetry) and Art (Cover Art with runner-up submissions as Featured Art).

Literature Submissions:
Literature submission cycles are six weeks long (currently 7/15-9/30). We select 10-15 pieces of Fiction, Nonfiction, and Poetry based on insight, commentary, and quality. We encourage artistic liberty and authentic thinking!

Art Submissions:
Art submission cycles last two weeks (next open for the Issue 2 Cover from 10/1 - 10/14). We select an art piece to be featured on the cover of the issue. It will appear permanently on the website and several others throughout the magazine.

Contact us for questions!
